About Our Communities

Three distinct gaming communities united under one family — each offering a unique experience for passionate players.

Roblox

SKYMAY Community

SKYMAY is a vibrant Roblox community focused on creating immersive experiences and collaborative gameplay. It offers an inclusive environment where players can come together to explore new games, build creative worlds, and participate in community events. The community encourages creativity and teamwork, welcoming players who are passionate about game development.

Requirements

To join SKYMAY, players should be enthusiastic about Roblox, participate in events, and adhere to the community's code of conduct.

Minecraft Bedrock

Endercraft Server

Endercraft Server is a Minecraft Bedrock edition server that focuses on providing an exceptional gaming experience through custom maps, mods, and engaging game modes. The community is built around collaboration, creativity, and friendly competition. Players work together to create impressive structures and explore new environments.

Requirements

Players need a Minecraft Bedrock Edition account and a willingness to participate in the server's various game modes while respecting the rules.

Free Fire

Enderflame Clan

Enderflame Clan is a competitive Free Fire clan that focuses on bringing together skilled players for intense battles and team-based competitions. The clan hosts tournaments and events where players can showcase their skills and engage in friendly competition. The community values teamwork, skill development, and a positive gaming attitude.

Requirements

Demonstrate a strong command of Free Fire gameplay, exhibit teamwork skills, and be actively involved in clan activities and events.
